\section*{Objective}
Compute volume of a cone using a lambda function.
\section*{Input}
Radius \texttt{r} and height \texttt{h}.
\section*{Output}
Volume value.
\section*{Approach}
Use formula \(V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\) inside a lambda.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
