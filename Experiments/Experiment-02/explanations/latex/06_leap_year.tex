\section*{Objective}
Check whether a given year is a leap year or not.

\section*{Input}
An integer year.

\section*{Output}
Print whether the year is leap or not.

\section*{Approach}
Leap year rules:
\begin{itemize}
  \item divisible by 400 \textbf{OR}
  \item divisible by 4 and not divisible by 100
\end{itemize}

\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
