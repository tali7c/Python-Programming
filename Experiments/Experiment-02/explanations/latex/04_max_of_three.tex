\section*{Objective}
Find the greatest among three numbers (assume no two values are the same).

\section*{Input}
Three numbers \texttt{a}, \texttt{b}, \texttt{c}.

\section*{Output}
The greatest number.

\section*{Approach}
Track a variable \texttt{greatest} and update it after comparisons, or use nested
\texttt{if} statements.

\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
