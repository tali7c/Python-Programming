\section*{Objective}
Find the greatest among two numbers. If numbers are equal, print a suitable message.

\section*{Input}
Two numbers \texttt{a} and \texttt{b}.

\section*{Output}
Greatest number or \texttt{"Numbers are equal"}.

\section*{Approach}
Compare using \texttt{if/elif/else}:
\begin{itemize}
  \item if \texttt{a > b} then \texttt{a} is greatest
  \item else if \texttt{b > a} then \texttt{b} is greatest
  \item else they are equal
\end{itemize}

\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
