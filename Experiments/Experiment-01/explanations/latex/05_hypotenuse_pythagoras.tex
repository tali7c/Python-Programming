\section*{Objective}
Compute the hypotenuse length $c$ of a right triangle using:
$$c = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}$$

\section*{Input}
Two non-negative real numbers $a$ and $b$.

\section*{Output}
The computed hypotenuse $c$.

\section*{Approach}
Read $a$ and $b$, then compute $c$ using \texttt{sqrt(a*a + b*b)}.

\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
