\section*{Objective}
Find the sum of the first $n$ natural numbers.

\section*{Input}
A non-negative integer $n$.

\section*{Output}
Sum $1 + 2 + \cdots + n$.

\section*{Approach}
Use the formula:
$$\text{Sum} = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$
In Python, use integer division \texttt{//} to keep the result as an integer.

\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(1)}.
