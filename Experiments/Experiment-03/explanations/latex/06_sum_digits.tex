\section*{Objective}
Compute the sum of digits of an integer.
\section*{Input}
An integer \texttt{n}.
\section*{Output}
Sum of digits of \texttt{n}.
\section*{Approach}
Repeatedly extract the last digit using modulo and divide by 10.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(d)} where \texttt{d} is the number of digits.
