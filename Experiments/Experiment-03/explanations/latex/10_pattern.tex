\section*{Objective}
Print the given numeric and star pattern.
\section*{Input}
No input (pattern size fixed at 5).
\section*{Output}
The required pattern with numbers and stars.
\section*{Approach}
Generate left and right numeric parts and insert spaces and stars in the middle.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\(\mathcal{O}(n^2)\) for pattern size \texttt{n}.
