\section*{Objective}
Compute the sum of the series \(1 + 1/2 + \dots + 1/n\).
\section*{Input}
An integer \texttt{n}.
\section*{Output}
Sum of the series.
\section*{Approach}
Accumulate \texttt{1/i} for \texttt{i=1} to \texttt{n}.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(n)}.
