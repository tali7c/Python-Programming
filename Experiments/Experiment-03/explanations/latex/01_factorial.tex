\section*{Objective}
Compute the factorial of a non-negative integer.
\section*{Input}
An integer \texttt{n}.
\section*{Output}
Factorial of \texttt{n} or an error message for negatives.
\section*{Approach}
Initialize \texttt{fact=1} and multiply from 2 to \texttt{n}.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(n)}.
