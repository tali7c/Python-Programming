\section*{Objective}
Generate the Fibonacci series up to \texttt{n} terms.
\section*{Input}
A positive integer \texttt{n}.
\section*{Output}
A list of the first \texttt{n} Fibonacci numbers.
\section*{Approach}
Iteratively update two variables \texttt{a} and \texttt{b} for each term.
\section*{Time Complexity}
\texttt{O(n)}.
